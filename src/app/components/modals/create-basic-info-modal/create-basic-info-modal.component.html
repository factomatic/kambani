WELCOME TO THE FACTOM DID REGISTRAR

The Factom Decentralized Identifier (DID) registrar is a UI, which walks you through the steps of creating a digital identity.

The basic digital identity you are about to create consists of a single public/private key pair, which will allow:

  • you to sign messages
  • anyone else to authenticate that a message has been signed by your digital identity

The public key of the digital identity will be recorded on the Factom blockchain and can be used by anyone trying to authenticate a signed message coming from you. The private key will be recorded in an encrypted file that you must download and keep safely stored. It will allow you to sign messages with your digital identity..

You are now ready to start! Make sure to keep an eye for the tooltips along the way, as they will guide you through the procedure.
